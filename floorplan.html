Business Forums

Business Forum sessions take place Monday 17th till Thursday 20th September 2018

The Business Forums are a full component of the Technical Programme of the IWA WWCE2018 and provide a series of sessions where Sponsors & Exhibitors present their innovations and projects that contribute to shaping our water future.

They are held in walk-in theaters at the exhibition floor. Apart from delegates and exhibitor staff, trade visitors have access to these Business Forum sessions also.

Join over 9000 leading professionals and companies from across the water sector, including thought leaders from within and outside the water sector.

The IWA World Water Congress & Exhibition is the global event for water professionals covering the full water cycle. However, as the Congress rotates through cities and countries each event has an extra emphasis on issues of specific interest to the region.

Focusing on leading practice, innovation and solutions, the event provides new networking and business opportunities, and ensures maximum exposure between participants, exhibitors and sponsors. It connects you with the right people and the right solutions, fosters new collaborations and partnerships, and provides a platform for exhibitors and sponsors to raise their profile amongst leading water professionals and companies.
